\hmsubsubsection{Initials}{AL}{LN}
To the right of several headings in this report, the initials (tab.~\ref{tab:group_members}) of the authors and proofreaders of the corresponding sections and subsections have been included.  Although this is a group project, it is individually graded and reviewed. Accordingly, it is valuable to be transparent with regards to which member has contributed to each section of the report. The authors and proofreaders are separated by a vertical line, with the authors on the left, and proofreaders on the right. The proofreader is not responsible for the content, but rather confirms the general form of the section, and helps fix minor spelling and punctuation mistakes.
