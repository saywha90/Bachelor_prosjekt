\hmsubsection{Group members}{OAH}{UMA}

This project consists of five engineering students from two academic disciplines: 
Ole Aleksander Hageløkken, Umran Mohamad Ali, Farden Shaheed, Mohammed Alghazali, 
and Filmon Kahsay Tesfay. Four members specialise in Computer Engineering with 
a focus on Cyber Physical Systems, while one member specialises in Mechanical 
Engineering with a focus on product development. This combination was essential 
for delivering a complete system: the mechanical engineer led the physical design 
of the robot arm, while the computer engineers handled software development, 
hardware integration, and system testing.

Each member is presented in Table~\ref{tab:group_members}.

\hmsubsubsection{Initials}{OAH}{UMA}
To the right of several headings in this report, the initials shown in 
Table~\ref{tab:group_members} indicate the authors and proofreaders of the 
corresponding sections and subsections. Although this is a group project, it 
is individually graded and reviewed. It is therefore valuable to be transparent 
about which member contributed to each section of the report. Authors and 
proofreaders are separated by a vertical line, with authors on the left and 
proofreaders on the right. The proofreader is not responsible for the content 
itself, but rather confirms the general form of the section and helps correct 
minor spelling and punctuation mistakes.
