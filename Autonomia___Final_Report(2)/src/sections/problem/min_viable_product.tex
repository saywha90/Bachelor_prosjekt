\hmsubsection{Minimum Viable Product}{OAH}{UMA}\label{sec:mvp}

The minimum viable product was agreed upon at the end of the inception phase. It
is defined as a system that can, without manual intervention, detect at least one
red or blue ball in the workspace, move the arm to the ball's position, pick it
up, and place it in the correct container. A single successful end-to-end cycle
constitutes the minimum acceptance threshold. Everything beyond this --- multiple
consecutive cycles, robustness across lighting conditions, fault recovery, and
mobile platform integration --- is considered an extension of the MVP.
